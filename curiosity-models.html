Components of the Pelz et al.2015 model

There are four components to the Pelz model:

  1. the learning curve that the learner follows as he attends to the stimulus,
  2. memory decay rate, as a model of the learners short term memory limitations,
  3. the cost of switching attention between objects (0.5), and
  4. the amount of information that the learner knows about the objects at the start of test.
